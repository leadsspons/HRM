How to get your Bot Token & Invite URL

🇰🇷 한국어
Two things you enter in the Admin Console — here's where each comes from.

🔑 Part 1 · Bot Token (from BotFather)

A token is the bot's password. You create one bot and reuse it across all your group chats.
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather (the one with the blue checkmark). Open the chat.
  2. Send /newbot. Choose a display name (e.g. "Workidoki HRM"), then a username ending in bot (e.g. emirard_hrm_bot).
  3. BotFather replies with your token, like 123456789:AAH...xyz. Copy it — this goes in the Bot token field (or in .env).
  4. Send /setprivacy → pick your bot → choose Disable. This lets the bot read normal group messages (required for task tracking).
  5. Open each group → add your bot as a member, then promote it to Admin.
⚠️ Keep the token secret. Anyone with it can control your bot. If it leaks, send /revoke to BotFather to get a new one.

🔗 Part 2 · Group Invite URL

Optional — a shareable link to the group. The bot fills in the chat_id automatically once it's added.
  1. Open the Telegram group you want to register.
  2. Tap the group name at the top to open group info / settings.
  3. Choose Add Members → Invite to Group via Link (mobile), or Edit → Invite Links (desktop).
  4. Copy the link — it looks like https://t.me/+AbC123.... Paste it into the Invite URL field.
📱 Mobile: tap group title → Add Members → Invite via Link 💻 Desktop: group title → Edit → Invite Links
💡 No link needed? Just run /addgroup inside the group. The bot registers it and captures the chat_id for you.
