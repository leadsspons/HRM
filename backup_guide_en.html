📂 Part 1 · Find your backups

Hostinger automatically backs up your account — here's where to open it
  1. Log in to hPanel and open Websites → your site's Dashboard.
  2. In the left menu, click Files → Backups. Dashboard› Files›Backups
  3. You'll see two kinds of backup: Files backups and Database backups.
    hpanel.hostinger.com › Backups
    🗂️ Files backups
    Your website files (HTML, PHP, images…)

    🗄️ Database backups
    Your MySQL databases (.sql)
    Select

⬇️ Part 2 · Download a backup

Keep an offline copy on your computer
  1. Under Files backups, click Select.
  2. Pick the date you want (usually the most recent) → click Next step.
  3. Click Download all files. The archive downloads as .tar.gz.
  4. For a database, use Database backups instead — it downloads as .sql.gz.
💡 You can also browse the backup and download just one folder or file instead of everything.

♻️ Part 3 · Restore a backup

Roll your live site back to an earlier version
  1. Files: Files → Backups → Files backups → Select → choose a date → Next step.
  2. Pick the file or folder you need and click Restore files.
  3. Database: open Database backups → choose a date → Show databases.
  4. Select the database and click Restore, then wait until it finishes.
⚠️ Restoring overwrites your current files/database. Download a copy of the present state first, so you can go back if needed.

⏳ Good to know · How long backups are kept

Retention depends on your hosting plan
  1. Weekly backups are kept for 6 weeks.
  2. Daily backups (higher plans) are kept for 7 days.
  3. After that, older backups are permanently removed — so download important ones in time.
